Welcome

At Nikki Links, I aim to provide more than just a random assortment of links. Each one has been thoughtfully chosen based on its quality and the value it offers to learners, professionals, and enthusiasts alike. My selections reflect not only personal interests but also the needs of those who seek in-depth, reliable information across diverse topics.


While a significant portion of this site focuses on open-source projects—highlighting the power of collaboration and transparency—you’ll also find a wealth of resources that dive deep into networking protocols, server management, and cybersecurity best practices. These are essential for anyone working in or interested in modern IT infrastructures. Additionally, for those passionate about retro technology, there’s a dedicated section to the charm of retro PCs and gaming, paying tribute to the machines and games that paved the way for today’s advancements.


AI is another area of focus here, given its transformative impact on the tech world. You’ll discover links to insightful articles, tools, and discussions that cover the latest developments and explore the ethical, technical, and creative possibilities of artificial intelligence.


This site is not just about collecting links, it's about offering a trusted space where you can explore, learn, and connect with meaningful content. Whether you're searching for detailed technical guides, the latest open-source tools, or a bit of nostalgia from the golden age of computing, Nikki Links strives to deliver.
